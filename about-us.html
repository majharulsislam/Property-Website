Rent

Sell

History of LREO

Established in 2008, LREO is privately owned and run by our founder Vic Chhabria. Proactive and approachable, we’re available to assist you wherever you are in the world.

Vic remains very much the head of ‘the family,’ guiding everyone with the enthusiasm, sensitivity and precision that continues to see him being sought out by primary home sellers, prestigious property developers and landlords the world over.
